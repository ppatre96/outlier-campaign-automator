## ICP — Precision Operator

**Who they are:** Detail-driven, process-oriented contributors who thrive on structured, repetitive accuracy tasks. Self-disciplined, methodical, proud of doing it right the first time. The most reliable data collectors — catch small errors before they cascade, follow project guidelines to the letter, deliver consistent output session after session.

### Demographic

- Age: 21–40 (core 26–35)
- Gender: mixed, slight male skew with strong female representation — target both
- Location: urban / semi-urban Mexico (CDMX metro per zip list)
- OS: Primarily Windows, some macOS
- Household: 1–4 people (18+), stable living
- Exercise: 3–4×/week (correlates with discipline trait)

### Psychographic — high-score signal traits

- Reviews all project guidelines before starting
- Detects small misalignments (quantities, labels, sequences)
- Creates personal checklists for repeated tasks
- Catches problems early and flags proactively
- Keeps files/materials organized and findable
- Labels and groups things for repeatable setup
- Leaves workspace tidy
- Consistent across days (low variance)
- Comfortable with careful repetitive steps
- Prefers clear "done" standards
- Asks clarifying questions upfront

### What motivates them

- Mastery over volume
- Clear, stable rules — ambiguity is friction, not challenge
- Seeing the impact of their precision (quality metrics, ratings)
- Predictable, consistent work availability over one-off high payouts
- Autonomy within structure
